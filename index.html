Our Goal

A non-profit organisation establised by four Professional Associations


ACCP registers membership among health professionals working in different section of physiology with in Australia. ACCP purpose is accrediting and regulating its members to practice in the Allied Health Professional Group of Clinical Physiology, and supporting communication about, and the advancement of Clinical Physiology Professions.
