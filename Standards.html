Standards of Conduct

  • Act in the best interests of the patients, clients and users
  • Respect the confidentiality of the patients, clients and users
  • Maintain high standards of personal conduct
  • Provide any important information about conduct, competence or health

Standards of Ethics

  • Carry out your duties in a professional way
  • Behave with integrity and honesty
  • Follow out guidelines for how to advertise services
  • Make sure behavior does not damage profession’s reputation

Standards of Performance

  • Keep professional knowledge, skills and experience up to date
  • Act within the limits of knowledge, skills and experience and, if necessary, refer on to another professional
  • Maintain proper and effective communications with patients, clients, users, careers and professionals
  • Effectively supervise tasks you have asked others to carry out for you
  • Get informed consent to give treatment (except in an emergency)
  • Keep accurate patient, client or user records
  • Deal fairly and safely with the risks of infection
  • Limit work or stop practicing if performance or judgement is affected by health
